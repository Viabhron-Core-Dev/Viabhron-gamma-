*   **Kernel Selection**: The **Synthesis Architect** selects the required SIS Kernels (e.g., *Narrative* and *Systems* for a writing app).

### 2. Manifest Generation
*   **PWA Manifest**: The OS generates a `manifest.json` for the Seed, defining its name, icons, and "Sovereign URL."

*   **Handshake**: The Mother Ship performs an **8004 Handshake** with the host device.
*   **Installation**: The user clicks "Install" (or "Add to Home Screen") to sprout the Seed onto their local hardware.

### 4. Resonance Sync

*   **Metabolic Tracking**: All activity within the Seed is reported back to the **Fiscal Comptroller** for metabolic auditing.
